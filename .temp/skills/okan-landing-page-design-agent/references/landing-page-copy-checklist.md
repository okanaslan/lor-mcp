# Landing Page Copy Checklist

Use this when writing or reviewing public landing-page copy.

## Copy Rules

- Lead with a concrete user benefit.
- Keep headlines short and specific.
- Translate product features into public-facing outcomes.
- Keep AI wording factual and user-controlled.
- Avoid unsupported claims such as guaranteed results, full automation, unavailable platforms, or launch dates not provided by the user.
- Prefer app-store-safe language and avoid exaggerated productivity promises.
- Keep legal/footer links plain and discoverable.

## Content Checks

- Product name is consistent.
- Store/download CTAs match platform availability.
- Feature claims match current or explicitly planned product behavior.
- Privacy, terms, and public utility links are present when needed.
- FAQ answers are short and factual.
- Coming-soon states are clearly labeled when downloads are not live.
