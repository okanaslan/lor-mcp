# Screenshot QA

Use this before finalizing store screenshot designs.

## Visual Checks

- Headlines are readable when zoomed out.
- Each screenshot has one clear message.
- Product screen content is realistic and not misleading.
- No text is clipped, collapsed, or too close to frame edges.
- Device mockups and app screenshots are aligned and consistent.
- App Store and Google Play sets are clearly separated and named.

## Claim Checks

- Feature claims match current or explicitly planned product behavior.
- AI claims are factual and user-controlled.
- Pricing, subscription, platform, privacy, automation, and guarantee claims are not invented.
- Coming-soon or placeholder states are clearly labeled if used.

## Delivery Notes

Report:

- Created or updated screenshot frames.
- Screenshot sequence and message per frame.
- Product screens or mockups used.
- Any claims or app states needing owner confirmation.
- Any export-size assumptions.
