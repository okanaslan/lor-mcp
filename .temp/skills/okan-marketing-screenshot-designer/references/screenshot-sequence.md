# Screenshot Sequence

Use this when planning App Store or Google Play screenshot story order.

## Default Sequence

- Screenshot 01: broad product promise and strongest value proposition.
- Screenshot 02: core daily workflow or primary use case.
- Screenshot 03: second major product area or differentiator.
- Screenshot 04: proof of depth, such as reports, reminders, planning, tracking, or summaries.
- Screenshot 05: supporting feature, trust/control message, or final CTA/download context.

## Rules

- One primary message per screenshot.
- Sequence from broad value to specific proof.
- Use AI, subscription, or automation claims only when the product actually supports them.
- Keep screenshots understandable without reading the full store description.
- Do not use fake app states that the product cannot produce.

## Headline Guidance

- Keep headlines short enough to read at store thumbnail scale.
- Prefer benefit-led language tied to a visible screen.
- Avoid generic claims like “be more productive” unless paired with concrete product proof.
- Use verbs and product nouns users understand.
